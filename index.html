Joey Android Apps is a one-person studio in British Columbia. We build focused, well-made apps for everyday life and health, and we keep your data minimal, secure, and yours.

About

We make a handful of apps we genuinely use ourselves. No ads, no tracking-for-tracking's-sake — just tools that do one thing well. Health and family-care apps are held to a higher bar: data is encrypted in transit, scoped to your household, and never sold.

Puffers — shared inhaler tracker

Make running out of inhaler medication impossible to do by accident, and end the "did the other parent already give it?" question. Every puff is a one-tap, append-only log shared across everyone who cares for a child — with dose reminders, refill alerts, and a count that's always right.
