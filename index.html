Our edge

An automated app studio, built on Claude skills

Every app you see below came off the same line. We've codified the whole lifecycle of an Android release into a set of reusable Claude Code skills — so the boring, error-prone parts (scaffolding, signing, store assets, policies, publishing) are automated, and the creative part gets all of our attention.

01

Scaffold

/new-project stands up a fresh app from scratch — project structure, signing key, store listing, privacy & terms — ready to build on day one.

02

Build & iterate

/build compiles a signed release APK & bundle on demand, with helpers like /convert-audio and /share-image bolting on features cleanly.

03

Add depth

/achievements wires up Google Play Games Services, leaderboards and achievements — the polish that makes a game feel finished.

04

Store-ready assets

Icons, feature graphics and screenshots are generated to spec (512×512, 1024×500, phone & tablet) — every asset on this page came out of the pipeline.

05

Publish

/publish pushes the app bundle straight to the Play Store, versioned and signed, with content ratings and data-safety answers prepared.

06

Stay compliant

Each release ships with a hosted privacy policy and terms at policies.joeyapps.abrdns.com — required by Play, done by default.
